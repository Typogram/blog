The Typogram Freebie Calendar: Curated Monthly Free Fonts for Designers

If you enjoyed this post, subscribe to get updates from our blog!

Typography lovers, rejoice! We’re excited to introduce a brand-new way to grow your font library and spark creative ideas: our Typogram Freebie Calendar — a curated freebie page that delivers awesome free fonts and design resources throughout the year. Think of it as a creative advent calendar, but instead of chocolate or ornaments, you get beautifully crafted fonts and type tools to use in your projects.

A Monthly Gift for Designers

Every month, we hand-select a special font or collection of font gems and make it available for free on our freebie page. 

These aren’t just random typefaces. Every month, we are curating these free fonts based on a monthly theme, and each font is thoughtfully chosen with designers in mind – so you get high-quality typefaces that inspire and elevate your projects!

Whether you’re working on branding, UI, editorial layouts, or social graphics, this curated calendar gives you new typographic tools to play with regularly. No guessing games, no endless searching, and no more worries about licenses – just great fonts ready to download and use for any projects. It’s like opening a gift from the Typogram community every month.

Why You’ll Love It

  • Curated Choices: Each font is selected to spark creativity and serve real design needs — from expressive display faces to elegant scripts.
  • Regular Inspiration: By releasing new freebies each month, we help you stay inspired and try new styles in your work.
  • Easy Access: Simply visit the freebie page and download — no complicated sign-ups or hoops to jump through.
  • Share the Joy: Found something great? It’s easy to share the freebie with fellow designers and friends.

How It Works

  1. Visit the Page: Head to https://typogram.co/freebie to see what’s currently available.
  2. Claim the Font: Add your favorite font freebie to your account and use them inside your projects, personal or commercial 
  3. Use Anywhere: Copy SVG or PNG or export projects from Typogram Studio for FREE to any tools you already use!

Stay in the Loop

To make sure you never miss our monthly free fonts, bookmark the Freebie Calendar and check back regularly. You can also subscribe to our newsletter for updates and share your favorite finds with the design community.
